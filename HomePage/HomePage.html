Our Menu

Welcome to Foodie's Nest, where every dish is a celebration of fresh, locally sourced ingredients and bold, innovative flavors. Our menu is thoughtfully crafted to offer a variety of options that cater to all tastes and dietary preferences, with a focus on quality and seasonal ingredients. Start your meal with our signature appetizers, ranging from crisp, refreshing salads to indulgent, hand-crafted dips. For the main course, you'll find a range of dishes that blend traditional favorites with contemporary twists—whether you're craving a perfectly grilled chicken tikka, a delicate seafood dish, or a vibrant vegetarian creation, there's something to satisfy every palate. We take pride in our desserts, offering a sweet end to your dining experience with decadent treats that showcase both creativity and craftsmanship. And don't forget to pair your meal with a selection from our carefully curated wine list, featuring local and international wines chosen to complement every bite. At Foodie's Nest, we believe dining should be an experience—a place where flavor, ambiance, and great company come together for an unforgettable meal.

About Us

Welcome to Foodie's Nest, where passion for food meets artistry in every bite. Nestled in the heart of Basavanagudi, our restaurant is more than just a dining destination—it's a celebration of the finest ingredients, bold flavors, and a dining experience that will leave a lasting impression.
